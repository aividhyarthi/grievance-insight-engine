About Us

Meet the Founder

The person behind the mission to democratize AI education in India

Rudra Kasturi

Founder, AI Vidhyarthi

Rudra Prasad Kasturi is a seasoned technology and digital strategy leader with deep expertise in building scalable businesses, AI labs, and growth strategies. With experience spanning Times Internet, India Today Group, CARS24, Yahoo, Amazon, and Snapdeal, he has been at the forefront of digital transformation in India.

An Electrical Engineering graduate from Madras University and a trusted advisor to companies globally, Rudra saw a glaring gap — students in tier 2 and tier 3 cities had little to no access to practical AI education. They were being left behind, not because of lack of talent, but because of lack of opportunity.

That's why he founded AI Vidhyarthi — to bring AI education directly to these communities, teaching in their own languages, and unlocking potential that the mainstream education system often overlooks.

Our Mission

Why AI Vidhyarthi Exists

Bridging the AI divide for India's next generation

The Problem

Millions of talented students in India's smaller cities and towns are excluded from the AI revolution. Language barriers, lack of resources, and no access to quality AI education keeps them behind.

Our Solution

We go directly to these communities, teaching AI tools and concepts in their own language — Telugu, Hindi, and more. Practical, hands-on learning that students can immediately apply.

The Vision

A future where every student, regardless of their city or language, can harness AI to build projects, launch startups, and compete on a global stage. Equal access to the AI future.

Our Core Values

01

Accessibility

AI education should not be a privilege of metro cities. We bring it to every corner of India.
