Glossary & Definitions
HIGH Severity
Complaints with significant volume (100+ mentions) and strong negative language. Likely causing brand damage and customer churn. Requires immediate action.
MEDIUM Severity
Recurring issues with moderate volume (10–100 mentions). Annoying but not dealbreakers. Worth addressing in the next product/ops cycle.
AI Engines
Negative signals found in Perplexity AI, Google AI Overview, and ChatGPT Search results — what AI assistants tell users about your brand.
Forums & Communities
Complaints from Reddit, Quora, and consumer forums — organic discussions from real customers often ignored in traditional reputation monitoring.
Review Platforms
Structured reviews from Trustpilot, Google Reviews, App Store, and Play Store — public-facing ratings that directly impact purchase decisions.
Social Media
Negative mentions on Twitter/X, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Instagram — often the fastest-moving signals of brand reputation problems.
Brand Health: Good / Fair
0–1 high severity issues detected. Isolated complaints exist but do not represent systemic problems.
Brand Health: Poor / Concerning
2+ high severity issues detected. Systemic problems that are publicly visible and actively hurting brand perception.
